# PNB Loans — Interest Rates & Eligibility

## Home Loan (Griha Rin)
- Interest rate: 8.40% per annum se shuru (floating, RLLR se linked).
- Maximum tenure: 30 saal.
- Processing fee: loan amount ka 0.35%, minimum Rs. 2,500 aur maximum Rs. 15,000.
- Eligibility: salaried ya self-employed, age 21 se 70 saal (loan maturity par).

## Personal Loan
- Interest rate: 11.40% per annum se shuru.
- Maximum amount: Rs. 20 lakh.
- Tenure: 12 se 60 mahine.
- Processing fee: 1% of loan amount.

## Car Loan
- Interest rate: 8.75% per annum se shuru.
- Financing: on-road price ka 85% tak.
- Tenure: maximum 7 saal.

## Loan Status
Apne loan application ka status jaanne ke liye application/loan ID chahiye. Status ye ho sakta hai: "under_review" (jaanch ho rahi hai), "approved", "active" (disbursed ho gaya), ya "rejected".
