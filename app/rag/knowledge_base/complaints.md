# PNB Complaints & Grievance Redressal

## Complaint Kaise Darj Karein
Aap complaint kai tarike se darj kar sakte hain:
- Branch mein ja kar complaint register mein.
- Customer care number 1800-180-2222 (toll-free) par call kar ke.
- PNB One mobile app ya net banking se.

Har complaint par ek unique complaint reference number milta hai jisse status track kar sakte hain.

## Common Categories
- failed_transaction: ATM/UPI se paise kate par mile nahi.
- card_block: kho gaya ya chori hua card block karwana.
- dispute: galat charge ya unauthorized transaction.

## Resolution Timeline
- ATM/failed transaction disputes: 5 se 7 working days.
- General complaints: 7 se 10 working days.
- Agar 30 din mein resolution nahi milta, to Banking Ombudsman ke paas escalate kar sakte hain.

## Charge-back
Unauthorized transaction turant report karne par (24 ghante ke andar) customer ki zero liability hoti hai — poora amount wapas milta hai.
