# PNB Savings Account — Charges & Rules

## Minimum Balance
PNB savings account mein minimum average balance (MAB) rakhna zaroori hai:
- Rural branch: Rs. 500
- Semi-urban branch: Rs. 1,000
- Urban / Metro branch: Rs. 2,000

Agar minimum balance maintain nahi hota, to non-maintenance charges lagte hain jo Rs. 50 se Rs. 250 per quarter tak ho sakte hain, branch category ke hisaab se.

## Interest Rate
Savings account par interest rate 2.70% per annum hai (daily balance par calculate hota hai, quarterly credit hota hai).

## Debit Card / ATM Charges
- Classic debit card annual maintenance charge: Rs. 250 + GST
- PNB ATM se cash withdrawal: unlimited free
- Doosre bank ke ATM se: 5 free transactions per month (metro mein 3), uske baad Rs. 21 per transaction.

## Cheque Book
Ek financial year mein 20 cheque leaves free. Uske baad Rs. 4 per leaf charge lagta hai.
